# Employee-Survey-Response-Dashboard-
Introduction

This project analyzes employee survey responses to measure overall employee satisfaction across different departments. The dashboard provides insights into employee engagement, satisfaction levels, and response trends, helping management identify strengths and areas that require improvement. It was developed using Power BI to present survey results in an interactive and easy-to-understand format.

Project Objectives

Analyze employee satisfaction levels.
Compare satisfaction across departments.
Identify the survey questions with the highest and lowest positive responses.
Provide actionable recommendations to improve employee engagement and workplace satisfaction.

Positive and negative response rates.
Overall satisfaction by department.
Positive response rate by survey question.
Overall satisfaction trend across survey questions.
Interactive filters for department and response scores.

Key Insights

A total of 15,000 employees participated in the survey.
The average satisfaction score is 2.98, indicating a moderate level of employee satisfaction.
35.89% of responses were positive, while 24.79% were negative, suggesting employees are slightly more satisfied than dissatisfied.
Employees clearly understand what is expected of them at work, as this question recorded the highest positive response.
Questions related to recognition, supervisor accountability, and workplace relationships received comparatively lower positive responses, highlighting areas that need attention.
Satisfaction levels are fairly consistent across departments, although some departments perform slightly better than others.

Recommendations

Strengthen employee recognition and reward programs.
Improve communication between managers and employees through regular feedback sessions.
Provide leadership and management training to help supervisors better support their teams.
Encourage employee engagement through career development opportunities and inclusive workplace initiatives.
Conduct regular employee surveys to monitor progress and measure the impact of improvement strategies.

Conclusion

The analysis shows that employee satisfaction is moderate, with positive responses exceeding negative ones. However, there are clear opportunities to improve employee experience, particularly in recognition, leadership support, and engagement. By implementing targeted improvement strategies and continuously monitoring employee feedback, the organization can increase employee satisfaction, boost productivity, and create a more positive workplace culture.
